Teams are required to register using the form below. Please submit one form per team, providing a single contact email address. Once you have registered, you will receive an email confirmation with a team ID. When the submission date approaches, you will be sent a reminder of your Team ID and instructions for submitting materials.

Registration will close 48 hours before the end of the submission window but we encourage you to register early if you are considering participating.

:::warning
The form below only registers you as a challenge participant. To present your work at the workshop, please also register for the workshop by visiting the [Clarity-2025 website](https://claritychallenge.org/clarity2025-workshop/).
:::
