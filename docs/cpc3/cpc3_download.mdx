The challenge data is available for download as a single 7 GB file. To obtain the download link, please fill in the form below and click 'submit'.

After submission, the download link will appear in the form, <b>directly on this page</b>. You should also receive an email titled "CPC3 Data Download Link" with the link. If you do not receive this email, please check your spam folder.
